About KGRCET

Located in Moinabad, KGRCET is dedicated in engineering India's Changemakers!
The college has been established by Aristotle Educational Society which was started in the year 2000 by young, highly qualified and motivated group of individuals with an intention to provide high quality technical education to students from rural and low socio-economic communities.
Since its inception, the society has established multiple and diverse institutions in the higher education space with a goal to graduate professionals who are trained and prepared to become leaders in their respective fields.
